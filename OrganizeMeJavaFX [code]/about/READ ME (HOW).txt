READ ME (HOW)
- How to run:
- download latest version of Java JDK. (version-25 or higher)
- download OrganizeMeJavaFX folder to your device drive
- double click Run.bat
- enjoy

----------------
- To access networking:
- double click on Server.bat on one device
- navigate to "Work Together" in the app
- change ip address from 'localhost' to your ipv4 address. (can be found in terminal by typing 'ipconfig'
- do the same for other devices
- enjoy
